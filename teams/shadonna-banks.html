Our Team

Shadonna Banks

Director and Founder

Shadonna Banks is a dedicated wife, mother of three, and grandmother of three, and is married to Attorney Elvis C. Banks. Residing in Mississippi, Shadonna is a passionate advocate for community empowerment and personal development. She is the founder and President of For the People, a nonprofit organization focused on helping individuals succeed in critical areas such as health, education, sports, music, and personal development.

In addition to her role at For the People, Shadonna is a Counselor in Training (CIT) at For the People Counseling, where she provides support and guidance to those in need. Her career in counseling and community service spans over a decade, during which she has held the position of CFO and Office Administrator at For the People Counseling Service.

As an entrepreneur and community leader, Shadonna is also the founder and Director of the Mid-South Cosmetology & Barber Association (also known as Beauty Barber School), where she is committed to providing high-quality education and training to students pursuing careers in the beauty and barber industry. She has extensive experience in the cosmetology field, having served as a director at the Tennessee Academy of Cosmetology for six years, and as a licensed instructor for over 23 years.

Mrs. Banks’ leadership experience extends beyond the cosmetology industry. From 2005 to 2009, she served as the Chairman of the Shelby County Head Start Program, where she played a pivotal role in ensuring early childhood education opportunities for underserved children.

Shadonna holds a Bachelor of Science in Applied Management from Grand Canyon University in Phoenix, Arizona, and is currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Mental Health from the same institution. She is also a Certified Immigration Specialist, further broadening her expertise in providing valuable support to diverse communities.

In 2019, driven by her passion for the beauty and barbering industry, Shadonna opened her own Beauty and Barber School. Her vision is to create one of the best online and in-person schools in the United States, offering top-tier education to students seeking to advance their careers in the beauty and barbering profession.

Shadonna’s ultimate goal is to continue making a meaningful impact on her community, empowering individuals to achieve success in their personal and professional lives. Shadonna is passionate about student success. As Mid- South Cosmetology  and Barber School grows she will be employing all of her diverse leadership skillset for the optimum student experience.
