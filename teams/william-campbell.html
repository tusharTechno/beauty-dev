Our Team

Mr. William. “ CC” Campbell

TN Licensed Barber Instructor

Mr. William Campbell is both an Arkansas and Tennessee Master Barber Instructor.  He adds a conglomerate of practical experience in hair restoration, design and artistry to our barber students. We refer to William as “CC” amongst the team here at Mid-South. Mr. Campbell has experience as a global platform artist showing off his barber education techniques to barbering students and the public across the world. “CC”  has had the previous experience of holding  a position as chairman of the Arkansas Artistry Association as well as being an ambassador for numerous global brands. When he is not in the classroom, you can find him behind the chair at CC Barbershop in the Little Rock area providing awesome men’s grooming services.
