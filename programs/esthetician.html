Overview

Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) offers a comprehensive Esthetician Course, designed to transform aspiring individuals into skilled, certified estheticians. Our course provides in-depth training in skin care, beauty treatments, and wellness practices, preparing students for a successful career in the thriving esthetics industry.
At MSBBA, our professional and licensed instructors are dedicated to guiding each student through every step of their journey. Whether you're interested in working in a salon, spa, or even starting your own business, our Esthetician Course provides the foundation and expertise you need to succeed.

The course spans 750 hours of training, covering a wide range of esthetic techniques and topics, including:

  • Skin Care Basics: Learn how to perform facials, skin treatments, and hair removal with precision.
  • Makeup Application: Master makeup techniques that enhance a client’s natural features for any occasion.
  • Advanced Skin Treatments: Understand the science behind advanced treatments like microdermabrasion and chemical peels.
  • Client Consultation: Develop the communication and interpersonal skills needed to build trust with clients and provide personalized care.

Our program integrates both theoretical learning and hands-on practice, ensuring students develop the technical skills needed to excel in the industry. While some components of the course are offered online for flexibility, the core focus is on practical, real-world experience. MSCBA’s instructors bring years of industry experience, providing valuable mentorship to students throughout the program.

Our Esthetician Course is designed for learners at all levels, whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills. Upon successful completion, students are fully prepared to sit for their state licensing exams, allowing them to become certified estheticians. Certification opens up numerous career opportunities, including the potential to start your own salon or spa.

Whether you plan to work in a beauty salon or establish your own business, the Esthetician Course at MSBBA prepares you for a successful, fulfilling career in esthetics. We are committed to providing high-quality, hands-on education that equips students with the skills and knowledge to thrive in the beauty industry.
