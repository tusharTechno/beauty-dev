Overview

Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy offers a comprehensive Barber Program designed for aspiring professionals who are ready to embark on a rewarding career in barbering. Our Barber Program, taught by highly skilled and licensed instructors, including experienced barbers, hair stylists, and color experts, equips students with the practical and theoretical knowledge needed to excel in the beauty industry.

Upon successful completion of the program, students will be fully prepared to sit for the state licensing exam and achieve certification as professional barbers. At Mid-South Cosmetology and Barbering Academy, we are committed to providing the highest quality education, ensuring that our graduates are not only certified but ready to thrive in the competitive beauty and barber industry.

Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) is dedicated to preparing future barbers with the skills, knowledge, and certification needed to succeed in the beauty and barbering industry. Employers consistently seek well-trained, skilled, and certified barbers, and our comprehensive Barber Program is designed to meet these needs by offering hands-on training and a strong theoretical foundation, all under the guidance of licensed and experienced instructors. The program is designed to build not only the technical expertise required to perform these services but also the professional behaviors and customer service skills needed for success.

Upon completion of the program, students will be fully prepared to take the state licensing exam, as they will have acquired the practical experience and theoretical knowledge necessary to pass with confidence. The MSBBA offers certification upon successful completion of the apprenticeship, giving students a recognized credential that opens doors to opportunities in barbershops, salons, and even the potential to start their own business.

At Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy, we are committed to creating a learning environment that supports students in building their careers and achieving professional certification. Whether students want to work in established barbershops or start their own salon, our program provides the foundation needed to succeed in this dynamic and rewarding field.

Our Barber Program is a rigorous, hands-on program, covers a range of essential skills that students will master consisting of 1,500 clock hours of in-depth instruction include:

  • Client Consultation: Developing the ability to assess client needs and provide tailored recommendations.
  • Hair and Scalp Treatment: Learning proper techniques for shampooing, conditioning, and treating hair and scalp.
  • Cutting and Styling: Mastering a variety of cutting techniques to create diverse and stylish looks.
  • Shaving and Shaping: Perfecting the art of shaving and providing clients with a clean, precise finish.
