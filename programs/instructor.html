Overview

Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) offers a specialized Barbering/Cosmetology Instructor Course, designed for licensed professionals looking to become certified instructors in the fields of barbering, cosmetology, esthetics, manicuring, and more. This unique program equips aspiring educators with the essential knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to teach and guide the next generation of beauty professionals. At MSBBA, we understand the importance of high-quality instruction. Our Instructor Course provides learners with comprehensive training in both the technical and teaching aspects of cosmetology. Our expert instructors, with years of industry experience, focus on helping learners develop the skills that are required.

The labor market for instructors is showing promising growth, and Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) has designed our Instructor Course with this in mind. We understand the increasing demand for skilled, knowledgeable instructors in the beauty and wellness fields, and our program is tailored to equip licensed professionals with the teaching skills necessary to meet this demand.

While many instructors choose to build their own businesses, the primary focus of this program is on preparing students to teach in established beauty, esthetics, and related institutions. Our course empowers future educators with the knowledge and experience to excel in the classroom, guiding the next generation of beauty professionals.
