Overview

At Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) we are committed to providing a thorough and immersive education that prepares students to become skilled and certified cosmetologists. While hands-on experience is essential in the beauty industry, we understand that modern learning needs flexibility. Our Cosmetology Course blends traditional in-person training with online resources, offering a comprehensive approach to education.

Though no cosmetology program can be entirely online due to the practical nature of the profession, MSBBA offers a hybrid learning model that combines live webinars, recorded sessions, and in-person training. Our online resources provide learners with a deep understanding of the science and philosophy of cosmetology, taught by our expert instructors who have years of experience in the industry.

MSBBA’s program is designed to ensure that students gain not only the technical skills required for certification but also the creativity and professionalism needed to excel in the competitive beauty industry. Our instructors are highly qualified and skilled, providing mentorship and guidance every step of the way. The course consists of 1,500 clock hours, meeting the State's requirements for licensure, and includes both theoretical lessons and practical training. While the online components allow students to learn at their own pace, they also participate in hands-on workshops and internships to gain real-world experience. This blend of online learning and practical training helps students build confidence and competence in the field.

In addition to the curriculum, we also provide apprenticeship opportunities that connect students with local businesses for the practical hours required for licensure. This ensures that our graduates have the experience and network to succeed in their careers.

At MSBBA, we believe in fostering creativity, technical excellence, and a strong professional work ethic in our students, preparing them for a successful career in the beauty industry. Whether you’re passionate about hairstyling, makeup, or overall beauty care, our Cosmetology Course provides a solid foundation for your career. The Cosmetology Course at MSCBA covers all the essential skills and techniques needed to succeed as a professional cosmetologist. Students will receive training in:

  • Hair Cutting and Styling: Learn how to cut and style all hair types to create personalized, beautiful looks for every client.
  • Makeup Application: Master makeup techniques, including the use of various brushes, products, and techniques for different skin types, to enhance natural beauty.
  • Client Consultation: Develop strong communication skills to assess and meet client needs, providing excellent customer service and satisfaction.
  • Hair and Scalp Treatment: Learn how to properly shampoo, condition, and treat hair and scalp for healthy, vibrant hair.
