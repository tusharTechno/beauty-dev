Overview

Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy (MSBBA) offers a comprehensive Natural Hairstylist Course designed to equip aspiring hairstylists with the essential skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the beauty industry. Our Cosmetology Course blends traditional in-person training with online resources, offering a comprehensive approach to education. This course is, taught by our highly qualified instructors, is designed to help learners become skilled professionals and earn their certification, preparing them for a rewarding career in hairstyling.

All services are performed by Future Professionals under the supervision of an instructor. Full-priced products are 100% guaranteed and may be exchanged with another product of equal or lesser value. Chemical services are guaranteed only when the prescribed products are purchased. We apologize, but for safety reasons, children are not permitted on the clinic floor unless receiving service. If waiting in our reservation area, they must be supervised by an adult.

At MSBBA, we believe that education goes beyond technique. Our instructors work closely with students to instill the values of hard work, dedication, and customer service, ensuring that they are fully prepared for the demands of the industry.

Upon successful completion of the program, students will be ready to take the state licensing exam and become certified hairstylists. The skills learned in our program open doors to a wide range of career opportunities, from working in established salons to starting your own beauty business.

Whether you're interested in working for others or launching your own venture, the Natural Hairstylist Course at MSBBA is your first step toward a fulfilling and successful career. Our program is designed to give you all the tools, training, and mentorship you need to thrive as a certified hairstylist.

Our Natural Hairstylist Program spans 300 hours, combining theory and hands-on training to ensure that students gain practical experience in key areas such as:

  • Hair Cutting and Styling: Mastering techniques for cutting and styling various hair textures.
  • Hair Dyeing and Lightening: Learning how to safely apply color and lightening treatments.
  • Permanent Waves and Design: Understanding different permanent styling techniques and design principles.
  • Facials and Makeup: Gaining foundational knowledge in skin care and makeup application to complement hairstyling services.
