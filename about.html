About Us

Home | About Us

The journey began in 2005 when we successfully opened a state-approved apprenticeship program that was then named Mid-South Cosmetology and Barber Apprenticeship School (MSCBA), an established association of cosmetologists, barbers, hairstylists, and salons. We then offered online and in-person apprenticeships in the beauty industry. The founder then saw the need to expand the services to offer more than apprenticeships, and then she sought licensure to be approved and was granted  full licensure as a Tennessee-approved school now called Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy(MSBBA), opening its doors at 3932 S. Perkins Road in Memphis in the Spring 2024 in the heart of the east Memphis community. MSBBA still does offer apprenticeship programs, which basically means that students can take their training in the beauty industry from our online instructions, then go to a salon or shop to complete their practical portion of their education and/or a student can take classes in our modern campus that includes a classroom and clinic floor. There is even the flexibility to do a mix of online and in-person as MSBBA seeks to be a leader in both in-person and distant. An enrollment official in our organization can help a student to determine which level of learning would be a perfect fit for their prospective education journey.
Mid-South Beauty and Barbering Academy is committed to promoting professionalism through skilled and experienced beauty professionals. The ultimate mutual objective of our initiative and the MSBBA is to provide potential beauty and tonsorial professionals with the right knowledge and perfect skills they need to shine in their chosen profession.
